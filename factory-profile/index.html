Expert Workers

Our factory employs 100–150 skilled workers — including dedicated QC staff, pattern cutters, and experienced operators — trained to produce consistent, export-quality headwear for global buyers.

On-time Delivery

Our standard production lead time is 35–45 days from order confirmation. We maintain buffer capacity to absorb demand fluctuations without compromising delivery schedules for existing clients.

Risk Protection

Every order goes through inline QC checks at cutting, sewing, and finishing stages. Buyers receive production progress reports and are welcome to arrange third-party inspection before shipment.

Transparency

We share real-time production updates and coordinate factory visits for buyers who wish to audit in person. No hidden charges, no surprises — what you see in the sample is what you receive at scale.
