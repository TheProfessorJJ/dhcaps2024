About the Company

Who We Are

Dhaka Hats & Caps is a headwear company headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are engaged in the design, development, and production of headwear for clients across multiple international markets.

Our operations are managed from our Dhaka office. We are a privately held company and have been operating for over two decades.

For business enquiries related to our international operations, clients typically reach us through our online contact form or directly by email and phone.
