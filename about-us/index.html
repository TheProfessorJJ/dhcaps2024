Our Story

We are a custom design hats & caps factory.

We started our journey during 1998. We are designer, manufacturer and exporter of any kinds of baseball hats, promotional hats, fashion hats and Beanie.

Our MOQ is 150 pcs Caps/Hats per color of fabrics. Our lead time is 15 days. We export all over the world from East to West and North to South. We work on custom designs basis and also for the promotional works.

We care about our quality of products as well as our customers all related factors like price issue, on time shipment, standard packing and suitable transportations system.
 

Why we are different

With over 30 years of Experience, our area of expertise is exceptional in Bangladesh. We provide competitive pricing and our manufacturing unit is professional with verified skilled workers.

The Caps/ Hats are solely made in our own Factory. We take orders from International customers worldwide. We accept MOQ 150 pcs or more Caps/Hats per color of fabrics. We make only premium quality caps/hats. Our daily production capacity is 6,000 pieces of caps/hats. We export anywhere of the world.
