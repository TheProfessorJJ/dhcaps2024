Questions

Frequently asked

Everything Norwegian buyers need to know before placing an order.

Do you ship custom hats to Norway?

Yes. We ship to Norway via DHL Express (3–5 days) or sea freight (18–25 days). We quote FOB Chittagong or CFR Oslo port — whichever suits your logistics.

What is the MOQ for Norwegian buyers?

MOQ starts from 144 pieces, varying by style. Our team will advise the right MOQ for your specific product requirements.

How long does production take?

Standard production lead time is 30–45 days from order confirmation and sample approval. Sample production takes 10–15 days.

What are the payment terms?

We accept TT (wire transfer), Western Union, and Wise. Standard terms: 30–50% advance on order confirmation, balance before shipment.

How are defects handled?

Every order is inspected against the approved pre-production sample before packing. Genuine manufacturing defects are replaced after verification.
